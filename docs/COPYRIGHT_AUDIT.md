# Copyright and redistribution audit

This public repository contains original prose, original scripts, factual
metadata, hashes, URLs, GUIDs, offsets, addresses, and minimal original
pseudocode. It does not contain firmware binaries, modules, executables,
disassembly exports, complete tables, or copied vendor manuals.

Vendor packages are linked and identified in `THIRD_PARTY_CONTENT.md` and
`evidence/FIRMWARE_PROVENANCE.csv`; they are not relicensed or redistributed.
The code/prose license split is described in `LICENSE` and `CONTENT_LICENSE.md`.

Contributors must supply only material they are entitled to publish.
