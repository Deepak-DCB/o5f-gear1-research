# Claim submission template

```yaml
claim: ""
evidence_class: "PROVEN | STRONG_INFERENCE | HYPOTHESIS | INVALIDATED | EXTERNAL_EVIDENCE_REQUIRED"
artifact_provenance: ""
artifact_sha256: ""
module_and_build: ""
address_or_offset: ""
source_and_destination: ""
condition: ""
minimal_reproduction: ""
raw_output_sha256: ""
publication_permission: ""
limitations: ""
```

Claims without provenance, stage, raw-output hash, or a reproducible method are
not accepted as project evidence.
